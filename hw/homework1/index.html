Unmanned Air Systems (UAS)

The atmosphere of technology and research today is pushing for smarter and more autonomous solutions to everyday problems and routines. One area of popular interest is Unmanned Air Vehicles (UAVs) operated remotely, which are part of a larger unmanned air system (UAS).

The area of operation, size of the unmanned vehicle, network resources and availability, and volume of other UAVs present, are all factors to consider when researching and optimizing a UAS. Something like this would require state of the art technology and a sophisticated network system to employ efficiently and safely.
