5G Communication for UAS


Typical UASs have incorporated ground-to-UAV communication techniques. Recent advancements have shown that integrating UASs into 5G cellular systems is a promising technology that can achieve valuable goals in terms of reliability, security, range of operation, and throughput (Zeng, 2019). Line of sight and other ground obstacles have traditionally been drawbacks with ground-to-UAV network communications.
